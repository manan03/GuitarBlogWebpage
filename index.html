Welcome to my blog!

Heyy ! Myself Manan Parihar , and i'm here to share you my guitar playing journey. So i bought this instrument in class 8th, and i started learning guitar by my own. Journey began by reading blogs , watching a tons of tutorials and spending most of the time in practicing. The process was fun but painfull too. A year passed and now i was able to play most of the basic songs in leads and chords. One day i watched Amin Toofani playing on youtube , from that day i started practicing 'gratitude'. Flamenco always had a great influence on my guitar playing. It's been 6 years now and i'm still learning by my own. This is how i moved from classical playing to blues and jazz. From Amin Toofani to John Mayer and Jimmi Hendrix.
